Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

OakNote respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled while using the app.

Information We Collect

OakNote stores your tasks, schedules, attendance records, settings, and preferences locally on your device. We do not collect personal information on our servers.

Notifications

OakNote uses local notifications to remind you about scheduled tasks. Notification settings remain on your device and are never shared.

Permissions

OakNote may request notification permission in order to deliver reminders. No other sensitive permissions are required for the core functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

Your information remains stored locally on your device unless future cloud features are introduced.

Children's Privacy

OakNote does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
