Introduction

OakNote is built with privacy as a core principle. Your schedules, attendance records, tasks, and preferences remain stored locally on your own device. OakNote does not require an account and does not upload your personal data to our servers.

Information We Collect

OakNote stores information such as your schedules, attendance records, tasks, reminders, notification preferences, and application settings directly on your device. This information is never transmitted to Diagle Labs.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored on your device is used solely to provide the features of OakNote, including attendance tracking, schedule management, reminders, and personalization of your experience.

Permissions

OakNote requests notification permission only to send local reminders for your scheduled classes, tasks, and events. No location, camera, microphone, contacts, or photo permissions are required for the app's core functionality.

Third-Party Services

OakNote currently does not integrate advertising networks, analytics providers, or cloud storage services. If future versions introduce optional online features, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features become available.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

Since your information remains on your own device, you retain control over your data. We recommend keeping your device protected using screen locks and regular backups where available.

Children's Privacy

OakNote does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no accounts or online data collection exist, information remains stored locally on the user's device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page together with a revised "Last Updated" date.
