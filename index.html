Privacy Policy

Your privacy comes first.
Last Updated • July 2026
OakNote is designed with privacy in mind. Your schedules, attendance, tasks, and preferences stay on your own device. We do not collect or store your personal information on our servers.

📂
Information We Collect

OakNote stores your schedules, attendance records, custom tasks, settings, and preferences locally on your device. No personal information is transmitted to our servers.

🔔
Notifications

OakNote uses local notifications to remind you about upcoming activities. Notification schedules remain on your device and are never shared.

🔑
Permissions

OakNote requests notification permission only to deliver reminders. No other sensitive permissions are required for the app's core functionality.

🤝
Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

🛡️
Data Security

Your information remains stored locally on your device. If cloud synchronization is introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

👶
Children's Privacy
